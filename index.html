Window Treatment Cleaning

We specialize on the “OnSite” cleaning of all window treatments. This includes: Honeycomb Shades, Fabric Verticals, Luminette Draperies, Silhouettes, Vignettes, Roman Shades, Pleated Shades, Pirouettes, Screen Shades, and ANY soft shades. These treatments are cleaned in your home where they hang without any removal or mess!

We cater to Residential and Commercial Properties

  • Custom made-to-order Bedding, Pillows, Draperies and Window Treatments

  • Color Consulting

  • Free In-Home Assessment and Estimates

  • Prompt and Professional Service

Lead times depend on the size of the job.  Custom Fabrication lead times are 3-6 weeks.
